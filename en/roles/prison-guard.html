Prison Guard

Difficulty: Medium

Duties: Oversee inmates, protect the Brig.
Supervisors: Head of Security
Руководства: Jurisprudence • Officership • Space Law • Smuggling • Security Inventory • Ten codes
A Prison Warden is a crucial role responsible for managing and guarding the Permabrig, a facility for holding highly dangerous criminals. Your duties include ensuring the safety of both the prisoners and the Permabrig itself, as well as preventing any prisoners from escaping or being exposed to external threats. The Warden wears a unique informal orange uniform.
