MEDICAL DEPARTMENT

Nanotrasen Space Station — Internal Documentation

OVERVIEW

Medical Department is responsible for treatment of injuries and diseases, production of medication, cloning of deceased crew members, storage and disposal of bodies, and determination of causes of death.

CHAIN OF COMMAND

Head of Department: Chief Medical Officer (CMO)

Superior Authority: Station Captain

PERSONNEL ROLES

Chief Medical Officer (CMO)

Coordinates the Medical Department. Responsible for personnel management, workflow optimization, and overall medical efficiency.

Senior Physician

Trains interns and inexperienced doctors. Oversees medical procedures and ensures protocol compliance.

Medical Doctor

Provides medical treatment, performs surgeries, and diagnoses crew members.

Chemist

Produces medical chemicals, medication, and various reagents required by the station.

Paramedic

Provides emergency medical assistance outside the Medbay and retrieves injured crew members.

Psychologist

Provides psychological assistance to station crew. Effectiveness is not guaranteed.

Intern

Junior medical staff. Performs minor tasks and receives practical training.

MORGUE STATUS INDICATORS

  • BLUE — Empty
  • GREEN — Body with active soul
  • RED — Body without soul or catatonic
  • YELLOW — Non-corpse object

NOTICE

Failure to comply with medical procedures may result in crew fatalities, station instability, or administrative action.
